South of the Old Yard looking north to Meeting House

Since 1838, this hallowed ground has served the original families and new residents of the Orrs Island community. The cemetery is owned and managed by a private association. The generosity of its members and friends provide for its care and upkeep. The binding ties of family and home assure that it shall remain a place of quiet reflection and fond recalls.
