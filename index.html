Orr's Island Cemetery, Orr's Island Maine

Since 1838, this hallowed ground has served the original families and new residents of the Orr's Island community. The cemetery is owned and managed by a private association. The generosity of its members and friends provide for its care and upkeep. The binding ties of family and home assure that it shall remain a place of quiet reflection and fond recalls.
