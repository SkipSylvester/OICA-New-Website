Grave and Memorial Stones

Summary of the Rules

All monuments and monument placement will be in accordance with the Orrs Island Cemetery Association Rules and Regulations. Monument sizes shall be determined by the size of the burial lot or plot the monument is placed on. Burial Right Grantees are requested, whenever possible, to use a single monument to identify multiple persons interred on their assigned burial lot or plot.

The location and position of a monument on a burial lot or plot shall be coordinated with and prior approved by the Superintendent. The Association requires that the carved or etched content on markers meet reasonable standards of decency and public acceptance. Without limitation this shall include graphic designs, symbols, pictures and lettered content. Grantees shall be responsible for the proper upkeep and maintenance of monuments placed on the lots or plots to which their rights apply.
